04 FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Memories to Music

How does image to song AI work?
Our AI analyzes your image's colors, composition, mood, and visual elements. It interprets the emotional atmosphere and generates unique song lyrics that capture the feeling of your photo. The process takes just 15-30 seconds and produces complete lyrics with verses, chorus, and bridge. You then take those lyrics to Suno AI to create the actual music.

How much does Memories to Music cost?
New users get 3 free credits to try the service. After that, you can purchase 25 credits for just $5.99. Each credit generates one unique song from your photo. Credits never expire, and there's no subscription required.

What types of images work best?
Any image works! Photos with clear emotional content like landscapes, portraits, weddings, sunsets, or meaningful moments tend to produce the most resonant songs. The AI finds meaning in colors, lighting, composition, and subjects.

How do I turn the lyrics into actual music?
After generating your lyrics, copy them with one click using our "Copy Song" button. Then open Suno AI (we provide a direct link). Paste your lyrics, add the style tags we suggest, and click generate. The whole process takes about 60 seconds.

What genres can I choose from?
Choose from 18 different genres including Indie Folk, Dream Pop, Lo-Fi, Alternative Rock, Ambient, Neo-Soul, Ballad, Synthwave, Bedroom Pop, Post-Rock, Art Pop, Slowcore, R&B, Jazz Fusion, Country, Cinematic, and Punk. Or let the AI auto-detect based on your image.

How long does it take to generate a song?
Song generation typically takes 15-30 seconds. Our AI analyzes your image, determines the mood and genre, and writes complete lyrics optimized for music generation.

Can I use the songs commercially?
The lyrics you generate are yours to use for personal projects, social media, or creative work. For music created on Suno AI, check Suno's terms of service regarding commercial usage rights.
