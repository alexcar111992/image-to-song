How to Use Suno AI (Step-by-Step)

1. GO TO SUNO

Visit suno.ai and create a free account if you don't have one. You get free credits to generate songs!

2. START A NEW SONG

Click the "Create" button on Suno's main page.

3. PASTE YOUR LYRICS

In the lyrics field, paste everything you copied from here. This includes the section labels like [Verse 1], [Chorus], etc.

4. ADD STYLE DESCRIPTION

In the style prompt, add the suggested tags that were included with your song (e.g., "indie folk, acoustic, melancholic").

5. GENERATE

Click "Create" and wait 30-60 seconds for Suno to generate your song.

6. LISTEN & ITERATE

Suno creates two versions. Listen to both! Not perfect? Try regenerating or adjust the style tags.
