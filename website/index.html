Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Image to Song AI

How does image to song AI work?
Our AI analyzes your image's colors, composition, mood, and visual elements. It interprets the emotional atmosphere and generates unique song lyrics that capture the feeling of your photo. The process takes just 15-30 seconds and produces complete lyrics with verses, chorus, and bridge. You then take those lyrics to Suno AI to create the actual music.

How much does Memories to Music cost?
New users get 3 free credits to try the service. After that, you can purchase 25 credits for just $5.99. Each credit generates one unique song from your photo. Credits never expire, and there's no subscription required.

What types of images work best?
Any image works! Photos with clear emotional content like landscapes, portraits, weddings, sunsets, or meaningful moments tend to produce the most resonant songs. The AI finds meaning in colors, lighting, composition, and subjects. Even abstract images can produce surprisingly deep and creative lyrics.

How do I turn the lyrics into actual music?
After generating your lyrics, copy them with one click using our "Copy Song" button. Then open Suno AI (we provide a direct link). In Suno, paste your lyrics, add the style tags we suggest (like "indie folk, acoustic, nostalgic"), and click generate. The whole process takes about 60 seconds.

What makes this different from other AI music tools?
We're the only tool that generates songs directly from images. Other AI music tools like Suno and Udio require you to describe what you want in text. We analyze your actual photos to capture their unique emotional essence and create truly personalized lyrics that reflect what's in your image.

What genres can I choose from?
You can choose from 18 different genres including Indie Folk, Dream Pop, Lo-Fi, Alternative Rock, Ambient, Neo-Soul, Ballad, Synthwave, Bedroom Pop, Post-Rock, Art Pop, Slowcore, R&B, Jazz Fusion, Country, Cinematic, and Punk. You can also let the AI auto-detect the best genre based on your image's mood and colors.

How long does it take to generate a song?
Song generation typically takes 15-30 seconds. During this time, our AI analyzes your image, determines the appropriate mood and genre, constructs metaphors from visual elements, and writes complete lyrics optimized for music generation.

Can I use the songs commercially?
The lyrics you generate are yours to use however you like - for personal projects, social media, or creative work. For the music created on Suno AI, please check Suno's terms of service regarding commercial usage rights, as that's generated on their platform.
