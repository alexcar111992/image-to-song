Sign Up for Suno AI

Step 1: Go to Suno.ai

Open your web browser and navigate to the Suno AI website.

Step 2: Click "Sign Up" or "Try for Free"

Look for the signup button on the homepage. It's usually in the top-right corner or as a prominent call-to-action button.

Step 3: Choose Your Sign-In Method

  • G Google: Fastest option - one click to sign in
  • D Discord: Great if you already use Discord

Create Your First Song

Step 1: Click "Create"

After logging in, find the "Create" button on Suno's main interface. It's usually prominently displayed at the top or center of the page.

Step 2: Enable "Custom Mode"

Toggle the "Custom" option to enable pasting your own lyrics. This gives you full control over the words in your song.

üí° Tip: Custom mode is essential for using lyrics from Image to Song!

Step 3: Paste Your Lyrics

Copy the lyrics from Image to Song and paste them into the "Lyrics" field. Make sure to include all the section labels like [Verse 1], [Chorus], etc.

[Verse 1]

Your coffee's cold on the windowsill

The morning light falls through the blinds

[Chorus]

I'm counting hours in the dust...

... and so on

Step 4: Add Style Description

In the "Style of Music" field, paste the style tags that came with your lyrics. This tells Suno what genre and mood to use.

indie folk, acoustic guitar, fingerpicked, soft vocals, intimate, melancholic
üí° Pro Tip: Keep it to 3-5 descriptive terms for best results. You can also specify "male vocals" or "female vocals" if you have a preference!

Step 5: Add a Title (Optional)

Enter the song title if there's a field for it. This helps organize your songs in your Suno library.

Step 6: Click "Create"

Hit the Create button and wait for the magic! Suno typically generates two versions of your song in about 30-60 seconds.

Usually takes 30-60 seconds...

Step 7: Listen & Choose Your Favorite

Suno creates two variations. Listen to both and pick the one you like best! You can download, share, or continue editing from there.

üéâ You just created your first AI song!

üí° Pro Tips for Better Songs

Be Specific with Style

Instead of just "rock", try "90s alternative rock, grunge, raw vocals, distorted guitar"

Specify Vocals

Add "male vocals", "female vocals", "soft vocals", or "powerful vocals" to your style description

Include Tempo

Add the BPM (beats per minute) from our lyrics for better matching: "indie folk, 75 BPM, slow and contemplative"

Iterate and Experiment

Don't like the first result? Regenerate with slightly different style tags. Each generation is unique!

Use Section Tags

Keep the [Verse], [Chorus], [Bridge] labels - Suno uses these to structure your song correctly
